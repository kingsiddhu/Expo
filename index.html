A GREEN TOMORROW

In October of 2023, the city of Doha will host the Expo 2023, an international Horticultural Expo that will gather millions of visitors from all over the world. Its main theme will be “Green Desert, Better Environment”. A global to the best minds and ideas to share innovation and knowledge about Horticulture and its ability to help mitigate desertification.
With a growing international consciousness on global warming and climate change, the hosting of a major Horticultural Expo within an arid climate is extremely relevant and is expected to have a profound impact not just on Qatar but also on the Gulf Region and beyond.

To embody a strong message in a brand

Representing a World Expo, the brand of Expo 2023 Doha Qatar would have to be clear about its meaning as well as striking in terms of visual impact and relevance. The brand would have to inspire a global audience, starting a fruitful conversation about crucial subjects.
Expo 2023 Doha Qatar will have four sub-themes: Modern Culture, Technology & Innovation, Environmental Awareness, and Sustainability.

A flower. A letter. A garden based on an idea.

The message of Expo 2023 Doha Qatar - involving the world in searching for better environmental solutions - is resumed in a challenging logotype. A unique flower made of seeds, desert, water, and sun. Inspired by the unique Arab geometry. All elements together compose the letter Q for Qatar. The logo of the Expo 2023 Doha Qatar™ is a colourful celebration of the fertile power of togetherness, a representation of when life flourishes over adversity. This was the visual DNA that would flourish and create an entire brand universe. The colour palette represents the natural tones of the desired ecosystems welcomed by the desert, presenting two main colours: Green, the colour of Nature, and Burgundy, Qatar’s national colour.

The Goals & The Impact

QATAR NATIONAL VISION 2030 is a compass for action, a horizon of sustainable governance and living towards every step counts. For every action a reaction is anticipated in a sequence of projects, collaborations and investments. Hera are a few of those steps forward. GOALS OF EXPO 2023 DOHA, QATAR The theme of EXPO 2023 Doha, Qatar is “Green Desert, Better Environment”. The goal is to encourage, inspire and inform people about innovative solutions for the reduction of desertification The Exhibition will offer an international platform for participants, stakeholders, decision makers, non-governmental organizations and experts to address the global challenge of “Desertification”. Holding an international horticultural exhibition in Doha is a real challenge and this is what makes it the perfect setting to discuss desertification. Global challenges require global action. EXPO 2023 Doha, Qatar will engage and unite international community to find solutions to desertification, whilst at the same time making a valuable contribution to achieving a sustainable future. Expo 2023 Doha, Qatar is aligned with the Qatar National Vision 2030, and is defined by the following objectives:

  • • To host the first horticultural exhibition in a region with a desert climate.
  • • To establish a showcase for ‘The Green City’ within the Middle East, by defining Qatar as a hub for this con¬cept.
  • • Encourage horticultural innovation focusing on the climate, water and the soils of Qatar.
  • • To promote Expo 2023 Doha, Qatar as a catalyst for international investments and business opportunities in Qatar.
  • • Highlight the synergies between landscape, sport and human health and well-being.
  • • To offer innovative actions which would allow humanity to combat desertification faster and more decisively before it is too late
  • • To constitute useful environmental outputs as a legacy for humanity
